## Problem Statement

Small-scale UK landlords (1-10 HMO properties) face:

1. **Spreadsheet Hell**: Managing room-level tenancies, rent tracking, and compliance across multiple HMOs in scattered Excel files
2. **Compliance Anxiety**: Gas Safety (annual), EICR (5-yearly), EPC (10-yearly), HMO Licence renewals - easy to miss with serious legal consequences
3. **Regulatory Uncertainty**: Renters' Rights Act 2025 changes (Section 21 abolition, PRS Database, Tenant Info Sheets)
4. **Maintenance Chaos**: Tenant requests via text/email/WhatsApp with no tracking or prioritisation
5. **Cash Flow Blindness**: No clear view of rent collection, arrears, or income per room

## Target Customer (ICP)

**Primary**: UK-based self-managing landlords with 1-10 HMO properties

**Characteristics**:
- Own Houses in Multiple Occupation (shared housing)
- Manage properties themselves (no letting agents)
- Tech-comfortable but not tech-savvy
- Time-poor, wearing multiple hats
- Compliance-conscious but overwhelmed

**Not for**: Large portfolio landlords (50+ units), letting agencies, commercial property
